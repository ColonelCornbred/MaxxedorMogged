Privacy Policy

MAXXED OR MOGGED

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using Maxxed or Mogged, you agree to the practices described here.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Account Information: Your first name, email address, age, and gender when you create an account.
  • Photos: Images you voluntarily upload for voting.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including votes cast and photos uploaded.
  • Device Identifier: Used for advertising purposes via Google AdMob.
  • Push Notification Token: To send you approval notifications and daily digests.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the app experience.
  • To display your uploaded photos to other users for voting.
  • To send push notifications about your photo approvals and activity.
  • To serve personalized advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To manage your subscription status via RevenueCat.
  • To moderate content and enforce our community guidelines.

🤝 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Advertising. May collect device identifiers and usage data for ad targeting. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In — Authentication only. We do not receive your password.

📸 User-Submitted Photos

Photos you upload are reviewed by our moderation team before being made public. Once approved, your photos are visible to all users of the app.

You can delete your photos at any time from your profile screen. Deleted photos are removed from our systems promptly.

Do not upload photos of anyone without their consent. By uploading a photo, you confirm you have the right to share it.

📢 Advertising & Tracking

We use Google AdMob to serve rewarded ads. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) to deliver relevant ads.

On iOS 14+, we will ask for your permission before using your advertising identifier via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings.

Premium subscribers see no ads.

🔒 Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data. Your data is stored securely via Supabase with encrypted connections.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

👶 Children's Privacy

Maxxed or Mogged is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

⚖️ Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • You can delete your uploaded photos directly from the app.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via your device's privacy settings.
  • You can disable push notifications in your device's Settings.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
